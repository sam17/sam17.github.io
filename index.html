About Me

Student

I am a final-year student pursuing Integrated Bachelor and Masters in Chemical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. My Masters thesis is on Decentralized aerial robots to track objects in 3D under Prof. Jayanta Mukhopadhyay , Computer Science and Engineering Department, IIT Kharagpur and Dr. Aamir Ahmad, Post Doctural Fellow at Max Planck Institute of Biological Cybernetics.

Home

I grew up in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India and am currently a resident of Rajendra Prasad Hall of Residence IIT Kharagpur, West Bengal, India.

Research

My research interests include Robot Vision and Multi-Robot Systems. I have also been recently introduced to Legged Robotics. I also am a keen follower of Deep-Learning research and would love to someday work on using it for robotics.
